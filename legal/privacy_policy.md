# Privacy Policy — FlagPost: Puzzle

Effective date: May 7, 2026

FlagPost: Puzzle is an offline casual sliding puzzle game.

## What this app does

- Works fully offline after installation.
- Does not require account creation or sign-in.
- Does not include chat or user-generated content features.

## Data collection and use

- No personal data is intentionally collected by the app.
- No location data is collected by the app.
- No advertising SDK is active in this version.
- No analytics, backend, leaderboard, or online profile system is active in this version.

## Local storage on your device

To support gameplay, the app stores data locally on your device, including:

- Puzzle progress records (best time, best moves, best stars)
- Daily streak values
- App settings (for example, sound preference)

This local data stays on your device unless you remove the app or clear app data through your device settings.

## Flag assets

Country flag images used in gameplay are bundled with the app as local assets.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future version, the updated policy will be included in the repository and app release materials.

## Contact

For support or policy questions, use the contact information provided on the app's store listing.
