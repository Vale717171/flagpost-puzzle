FlagPost: Puzzle Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

FlagPost: Puzzle is a simple offline puzzle game. This page explains what information the app handles and how that information is used.

What the app does

  • FlagPost: Puzzle is designed to work as an offline puzzle game.
  • The app does not require an account or sign-in.
  • The app does not use analytics.
  • The app does not access your location.
  • The app does not use a server backend for gameplay or progress storage.

Local device storage

Your puzzle progress, settings, and related gameplay data are stored locally on your device so the app can remember things like completed puzzles, best scores, sound settings, and streak progress.

This information stays on your device unless your device or platform backs it up using its own system features.

External support link

The app can open an external Buy Me a Coffee page only if you explicitly tap the support link in Settings.

Advertising

The current version of FlagPost: Puzzle does not include analytics or a server backend.

In the future, the app may optionally include ads delivered through Google AdMob. If that happens, this privacy policy should be updated to describe the ad-related data practices and any choices available to users.
